A few months after the launch of the 2015 MINI Cooper D 3-door and 5-Door, MINI has now launched the 3-Door MINI Cooper S at Aamby Valley, Pune. This exclusive driving event gave people an opportunity to get behind the wheel and experience an adrenaline rush unlike any other.

The new MINI TwinPower Turbo engine makes the new MINI faster and lighter on fuel.
Bustling beneath the bonnet of the new MINI Cooper S is the mighty 4-cylinder MINI TwinPower Turbo petrol engine delivering 280 Nm torque. Developed from scratch for the new MINI, it injects the new MINI Cooper S with 141 kW / 189 hp of pure unadulterated go-kart fun. It only needs 6.7 seconds to hit 100 km/hr and its top speed is a mind-bending 233 km/h. It’s not just a car, it’s an alluring athlete.

The new MINI Cooper S is now available at MINI dealerships across India as a CBU (completely built-up) unit.

Combine British character with Italian craftsmanship, and what do you get? A sensational open two-seater. MINI and legendary coachbuilder touring Superleggera go back to the basics of quality driving with an electric-powered speedster that’s light, sharp and agile. Elegance, with a unique sporting edge.

The MINI Superleggera Vision showcases traditional MINI design on a classic Speedster bonnet, with the iconic round headlights and hexagonal grille. Plating on the grille gives it sporting character, while three-dimensional bonnet stripes emboss the hood, leading to polished aluminium accents.

A wide stance and muscular wheel arches contrast against sleek aluminium mirrors and rims, finished off in eye-catching Como Blue paint. From behind, the striking rear lights form a two-part LED Union Jack, separated by a sparkling chrome bar. It’s a wave to the past, as you roar into the future.

The 8th edition of Asia’s largest literature festival, the Jaipur Literature Festival, was held from 17th-21st January, 2014 at Diggi Palace in Jaipur. It saw the presence of over 200 authors, journalists and bloggers including two Nobel laureates and a footfall of over one lakh visitors. Right in the thick of things was the MINI Art Installation by renowned photographer Rohit Chawla – an exquisite creation that featured the faces of key speakers from the festival’s 7th edition. Several eminent personalities such as Jhumpa Lahiri and Shobha De posed with the MINI Art Installation and it was featured in top publications like India Today Magazine and Mail Today. It also created a massive buzz in the digital world with over 4 lakh tweets and re-tweets by followers of celebrities and media channels.
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
Organised by Genesis Foundation, the two day Kasauli Rhythm and Blues Festival is an annual music festival held in Kasauli, Himachal Pradesh. This year, the fourth edition of the festival will be held from April 3 – 5, 2015 at Baikunth Resorts, and it will showcase artists such as Barefaced Liar, Sonam Kalra and Kutle Khan, Suneeta Rao, Whirling Kalapas, Rabbi Shergill and Swarathma among others.

Just like in 2014, MINI plans to partner with the Kasauli Rhythm & Blues Festival this year too. MINI’s association with this festival is a manifestation of its lively and spirited approach of ‘doing things differently’ and making it a spectacular experience for everyone – especially when done for a worthy cause of supporting critically ill children.
